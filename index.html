Program Dates & Format

  • Duration: 4 weeks (Residential, On-Campus)

Academic Focus

The institute introduces students to foundational and emerging ideas in modern sleep research, bridging neuroscience, psychology, human physiology, and data analysis.

  • Sleep architecture (NREM/REM), homeostatic regulation, circadian biology
  • Memory consolidation, learning, attention, and executive function
  • Sleep deprivation models and performance decline
  • Behavioral interventions and sleep hygiene frameworks
  • Wearable metrics, sleep scoring, and interpretability of sleep data
